Cordoba Kicks Off Golden Swing
The Cordoba Open makes it debut on the ATP Tour in 2019. It is the first clay-court tournament of the season and also the opening leg of the five-tournament swing through Latin America, aptly coined the "Golden Swing". The city of Cordoba, 435 miles northwest of Buenos Aires, has hosted a number of international sporting events, including FIFA World Cup football qualification matches.
