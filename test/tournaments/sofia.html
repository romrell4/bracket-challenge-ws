Tennis In The Capital
2019 Tournament: 4-10 February
Bulgaria has made its mark on the ATP World Tour through Grigor Dimitrov, and the country hosted its first ATP World Tour event in its capital city of Sofia in 2016. Fittingly, Dimitrov won the tournament in 2017 during his Sofia debut. The Sofia Open is staged at the Arena Armeec Sofia, Bulgaria’s leading sports and entertainment venue.
